swiggy

Swiggy, founded in 2014, is a leading Indian on-demand convenience platform focused on food delivery, groceries, and other essential services. Through its unified app, Swiggy connects millions of consumers with restaurant partners and retailers via a vast network of delivery partners. Key services include Swiggy Instamart (quick commerce for groceries), Swiggy Dineout (restaurant bookings and deals), and Swiggy Genie (a concierge service), all contributing to its mission to enhance urban consumer lifestyles through unparalleled convenience.

Mission and Approach:

  • Mission: To elevate urban consumers' quality of life by providing unparalleled convenience through its platform and services.
  • Technology-Driven: Leverages cutting-edge technology for its operations and user experience.
  • Comprehensive Platform: Offers a unified app integrating multiple services, creating a complete convenience solution for users.
  • User & Partner Ecosystem: Collaborates with a vast network of over 2.5 lakh restaurants and 5.4 lakh delivery partners.

    • History & Development:

  • 2014: Launched as a food delivery service.
  • 2020: Expanded into quick commerce with Swiggy Instamart.
    • Ongoing Innovation: Continues to introduce new features and standalone services, such as Swiggy Scenes and Snacc, to target new market segments.
